Managed Infra

Meraqui is the leader and expert in providing end-to-end, integrated human capital solutions for the infrastructure sector. We have the experience, expertise, and technology to offer SLA-based projects and allied workforce management solutions for your infra needs.

  • Manage the workforce for operations and maintenance of your infra projects, such as telecom towers, power plants, solar farms, and more. We have a large network of skilled and experienced infra workers who can handle your project requirements efficiently and effectively.

  • Ensure compliance with safety and environmental standards as per telecom and infra regulations, such as EMF, OHSAS, ISO, and others. Our team of experts monitor, audit, and report the safety and environmental performance of your infra projects.

  • Track and optimize your IT asset management, such as hardware, software, licenses, and warranties. We use a cutting-edge IT asset management system that effectively handles the entire lifecycle of your IT assets, and improve their usage, performance, and security.

Our infra solutions are powered by state-of-the-art technology, compliance knowledge, and strategic thinking. We use advanced tools and platforms to source, screen, and onboard infra workers, as well as to track, measure, and optimize their performance. Confidently build and operate your infrastructure, knowing that we have the expertise and the technology to deliver the best solutions for your infratel projects. Contact us today to know more.
