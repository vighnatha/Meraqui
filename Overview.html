About Meraqui

Who we are

We are a tech-enabled workforce management platform, specializing in employee lifecycle management, job and requisition management, and training and interview assessments. We link our expertise with your business demands and proactively begin HR procedures to meet your needs, harnessing the power of Artificial Intelligence. We have an active & engaging presence in 3 Countries, 26 states and 135 cities, while still bringing that local flair to you when it comes to sourcing strength.
